Marinated Tuna Steak

This is the best, easy tuna steak marinade! All you need is a few simple ingredients and 30 minutes to infuse
your tuna steaks with deliciously bold flavor –zesty, with fresh herbs, garlic, a hint of sweet honey, and savory spices.
Tuna steak is one of my favorite fish to make. It’s so meaty and easy to cook and tastes delicious. This marinade for tuna steaks
couldn’t be any easier – it’s full of simple ingredients you probably already have (think oil, honey, garlic, cumin, parsley).
