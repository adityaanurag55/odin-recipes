Broiled Lobster Tails

lobster tail is a dish of lobster tails cooked on a grill and basted in a buttery herb sauce.
It's a BBQ-inspired dish that results in tender lobster meat with a smoky flavor.
Grilled lobster tails seasoned with lemon, olive oil, garlic, and paprika.
This recipe takes no time at all but the lobster is oh-so-tender and delicious!

Ingredients

  • Lobster tails: You'll need two whole lobster tails.
  • Butter: A stick of melted butter is essential for this rich lobster recipe.
  • Seasonings: These lobster tails are simply seasoned with salt, pepper, and paprika.
  • Lemon: A fresh lemon, cut into wedges, adds welcome brightness.

Directions

  1. Using kitchen shears, cut the tops of the lobster tails lengthwise and pull apart the shells.
  2. Coat the meat with butter and season with the salt, pepper, and paprika.
  3. Broil the lobster until lightly browned and serve with lemon wedges.
