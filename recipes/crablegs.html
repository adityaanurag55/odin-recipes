Garlic Crab Legs

An easy crab legs recipe with a garlicky butter sauce that's ready in just 20 minutes!
I ended up getting some snow crab legs on sale at the market. I wanted something a
little different from plain steamed, boiled, or grilled crab legs,so I ended up
throwing a few things together to make this awesome crab leg recipe.
This dish would be great with shrimp as well.
